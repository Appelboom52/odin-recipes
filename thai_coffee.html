Thai coffee

Description

Thai coffee, as is served in Thai restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons ground coffee beans
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground cardamon
  • water
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Place coffee and cardamon in the filter of your coffee machine. Place enough water to make 2 cups of coffee in the machine. Turn on the coffee machine.
  2. Pour brewed coffee into 2 coffee cups, and stir 1 tablespoon sweetened condensed milk into each cup. Serve.
